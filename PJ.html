What is an Economic system?

-INTRODUCTION: Economic system is the relationship between the components of an economic, such as households, firms and government, and the institutional arrangement within which they operate. It governs the solving of economic problems of the society such as;

  1. What to produce
  2. How to produce
  3. For whom to produce and resources to employ

    4. There are three basic economic system in the world. They are;

    1. CAPITALISM/FREE MARKET ECONOMY:-
      2. This is an economic system characterized by private ownership of meansof production and distribution and their operation for profit.

    2. SOCIALISM ECONOMY:-
      3. This is an economic system in which the means of distribution & production are owned and controlled by the government. It can also be known as "Centrally planned system".

    3. MIXED ECONOMY:-
      4. This is an economic system in which the means of production & distribution are in both the private and public sectors. It is simply the combination of both public and private sectors.
